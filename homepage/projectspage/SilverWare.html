- SilverWare is an application that streamlines different functionalities in restaurant management. These functions include creating restaurant menus, table layouts, queues, waitlists, employee management, and storing customer orders. The software aims to provide tools to help managers and employees in the restaurant industry do their jobs more efficiently.
- Having one piece of software that assists employees across an entire restaurant makes it easier for both employees and owners, as every task they need to complete is handled through SilverWare. Restaurant owners can appreciate that all orders are logged for analytics, and our average wait time and table check-up time data can be used as specific training points.
